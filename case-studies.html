Built an AI Monitoring Framework That Cut Errors from 15% to Under 2%

Developed a 4-step quality framework for AI chatbot systems that dramatically reduced overpromises and hallucinations — turning an unreliable AI product into one that customers could trust.

The Challenge

AI chatbots were making promises the product couldn't keep in 15% of conversations — creating support escalations, eroding trust, and threatening retention. The team had no systematic way to detect, measure, or reduce these failures.

The Approach

Designed and implemented a 4-step monitoring and intervention framework: systematic detection of overpromise patterns, real-time flagging, automated guardrails, and continuous feedback loops. This wasn't just a technical fix — it required rethinking how the product team defined and measured AI quality.
